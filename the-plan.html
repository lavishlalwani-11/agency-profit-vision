The Plan: Agency Profitability at Scale

Our roadmap to transform Ailo from software vendor to strategic business partner

This document outlines how we'll fundamentally realign our business model with what agencies actually care about: profitability, growth, and operational excellence.

What We're Building

True partnership means that Ailo is building an operating system for agencies‚Äîa comprehensive framework that shapes how agencies think, operate, and grow. This goes beyond software. It's about becoming the foundation upon which winning agencies are built.

This isn't about adding features. It's about fundamentally realigning our business model with what agencies actually care about: profitability, growth, and operational excellence.

Why This Matters

Market Misalignment

Agencies struggle to connect software spend to business outcomes. Current solutions don't address what actually drives agency success.

Our Current Position

We're seen as a "tech provider" not "strategic partner." This limits pricing power, customer relationships, and long-term value.

The Unlock

If we can demonstrably drive agency profitability, we open new monetization models and dramatically increase customer lifetime value.

Competitive Landscape

No one else is positioned as a true business operating system. This is a significant strategic opportunity.

Revenue Potential

Success-based pricing, premium tiers, and deeper relationships = exponential value vs. traditional per-property SaaS model.

The Roadmap

Phase 1: Set Up for Transformation

Goal: At the end of this phase, Ailo's team, product, pricing, and go-to-market should be ready for agency profitability at scale.

‚ñº

A. Developing a Shared North Star

Build Measurement Infrastructure

  • Instrument the product to capture core agency profitability metrics
  • Create real-time dashboards visible to the entire Ailo team
  • Establish baseline metrics for all current customers
  • Build attribution models connecting Ailo usage to outcomes

Embed Core Metrics in Product

Make these metrics visible and actionable within the platform:

  • Customer satisfaction (Owner NPS, Tenant NPS)
  • PM satisfaction and retention
  • Efficiency: Properties per PM ratio
  • Portfolio growth and churn rates
  • Management profitability/fees (average fee %, margin %)
  • Arrears rates and trends
  • Response time metrics
  • Portfolio value and quality indicators

Make Metrics Transparent

  • Every team at Ailo should see agency success metrics
  • Weekly/monthly reviews of customer health tied to these metrics
  • Success stories tied to specific metric improvements

B. Building the Operating System: Team Transformation

Retrain GTM Teams on Core Metrics and Profitability

Account Management:

  • Train AMs on agency profitability drivers and core metrics
  • Build AM dashboards showing their agencies' health across all metrics
  • Shift AM role from "support" to "strategic coach"
  • AMs help agencies set goals during onboarding, then coach them toward achievement
  • Quarterly business reviews focused on outcomes, not features
  • Clear attribution: "Here's how your metrics improved and how Ailo contributed"

Enablement:

  • Retrain on core metrics and how to move the needle
  • Develop coaching frameworks and playbooks
  • Focus on outcome-driven onboarding (not just feature training)
  • Capture agency intent and goals upfront
  • Create success paths for different agency types

Marketing:

  • Refocus content strategy on thought leadership
  • Publish agency profitability insights, benchmarks, and research
  • Run events showcasing transformation stories (not features)
  • Generate leads through coaching and expertise, not just product demos
  • Goal: "Agencies come to us because we make them better, not just more digital"

C. Product Development Priorities

Area 1: Create a Pain-Free Product Experience

Get the fundamentals right before pushing transformation messaging.

Support & Stability:

  • Get support under control (reduce ticket volume, improve response times)
  • Address "I just can't do something so simple" issues that create daily friction
  • Examples: Default inspection templates, CC/FWD functionality in messages, other critical pain points

Important Note: We won't completely eliminate all issues, but we need them under control. Agencies can't see us as a strategic partner if basic operations are painful.

Area 2: Continue Innovation with Ailo Accelerate Features

Keep pushing the platform's value and differentiation:

  • Customer sentiment analysis
  • AI summary and conversation intelligence
  • AI keys (automated data extraction and classification)
  • Other AI-powered automation features

Why This Matters: We need to continue innovating to justify premium tiers and maintain competitive advantage. Can't just fix problems‚Äîmust also demonstrate future vision.

Area 3: Close the Loop for Sales (Growth Module)

Complete the end-to-end agency lifecycle:

First Priority: Renewed focus on Growth module

  • Lead capture and tracking
  • Conversion pipeline
  • Landlord onboarding workflows

Second Priority: Leasing workflows

  • Tenant acquisition
  • Application processing
  • Seamless handoff to management

Why This Matters: Agencies need growth tools to achieve profitability outcomes. Can't just help them manage better‚Äîneed to help them grow.

D. Pilot Partnership Model

  • After shared dashboard is live, pilot Partnership success-based pricing with 5 agencies
  • Set clear goals with each pilot agency upfront
  • Track attribution: which Ailo interventions drive which outcomes
  • Document learnings from each pilot
  • Validate pricing models and operational approach

Phase 1 Key Deliverables

  • Measurement infrastructure live and shared dashboards operational
  • GTM teams retrained and equipped
  • Core/Accelerate tiers launched
  • Partnership pilots running with clear goals
  • First case studies in progress

Phase 2: Practice & Prove the Model

Goal: Practice agency profitability at scale and prove the model works. At the end of this phase, our teams should be consistently getting wins on the board in driving agency profitability. We should start getting referrals and market mentions from our success.

‚ñº

A. GTM Teams Operating Harmoniously Around Core Metrics

Consistent Success with Continuous Learning

  • Teams operating with shared language and focus on core metrics
  • Test and learn fast from failures‚Äîwhether product features, coaching techniques, or data approaches
  • Document all learnings systematically (what works, what doesn't, why)
  • Share successes and failures across teams in real-time

AI-Assisted Account Management at Scale

  • AI helps AMs prioritize which agencies to coach based on risk indicators, opportunity indicators, and engagement patterns
  • AI provides clear coaching points for each agency interaction
  • AMs focus time where they can have highest impact

Building the Evidence Base

  • Customer stories consistently published (aim for 2-3 per month)
  • Stories shared at community events and webinars
  • Focus on clear attribution: "Here's what changed and how Ailo contributed"
  • Agencies starting to refer others based on outcomes

B. Scale Partnership Model

Validate and Expand

  • Evaluate Phase 1 pilot results
  • Refine success-based pricing formulas based on learnings
  • Onboard more agencies into Partnership tier (target: 20-30 agencies)
  • Build operational playbook for Partnership management
  • Create Partnership tier go-to-market approach

Partnership Operations

  • Dedicated account management for Partnership agencies
  • Regular business reviews (monthly or quarterly)
  • Clear success metrics tracked for each partnership
  • Refined pricing models based on pilot data

C. Advanced Accelerate Features & AI Agents

Continue Innovation

Develop and launch advanced AI capabilities:

  • AI Mode: Conversational interface for platform interaction
  • AI Team Coaching: Real-time performance feedback for PMs
  • AI Insights & Guidance: Proactive recommendations pushed to users

Deploy AI Agents with Clear Attribution

"Partner with us to manage your agency"

Growth Agent (Free): Lead nurture and conversion automation. Free because it helps both Ailo and agency grow (Win-Win). Drives portfolio growth that benefits everyone.

Repairs Agent (Revenue Share): End-to-end repairs management. Agency makes money from tradie referral fees/cuts. Ailo takes a clip of the value created.

Compliance Agent (Revenue Share): Manage all compliance through digital certificates. Price of compliance today + makes additional money. Ailo takes a clip of the compliance revenue.

Staff Performance Agent (Value-Based): Helps team do their work better. Coaching and motivating staff in real-time. Boosts value and saves time. Pricing based on team size or value saved.

Agent Attribution: Each agent must show clear before/after impact on agency metrics.

D. Close the Circle (Lower Priority)

Commercial Property Support: Extend platform capabilities to commercial properties, addressing gap in current offering. Secondary priority behind proving residential model.

Phase 2 Key Deliverables

  • GTM teams consistently driving agency profitability wins
  • 15-20 published case studies with clear attribution
  • AI agents launched with clear value models
  • Partnership tier operational (20-30 agencies)
  • Market referrals and mentions beginning
  • Documented playbooks from learnings
  • Advanced Accelerate features live

Phase 3: Scale & Lead

Goal: Become the recognized leader in agency profitability. Scale the model across the customer base. Agencies compete to share their P&Ls with us and partner with us.

‚ñº

A. Market Leadership

  • Position as thought leader in agency profitability
  • Regular industry research and benchmarking
  • Major annual event (conference/summit)
  • Media and PR campaign
  • Community becomes industry hub
  • Agencies actively want to partner: Sharing P&Ls becomes badge of honor
  • Partnership tier seen as exclusive club that agencies compete to join

B. Scale Operations

  • Expand coaching team (20+ coaches)
  • Onboard 50+ Partnership agencies (selective, competitive process)
  • Tier distribution target: 40% Core, 40% Accelerate, 20% Partnership
  • Revenue per agency increases 3-5x over per-property model
  • Agencies proactively approaching us to share financials and prove readiness for Partnership

C. Product Maturity

  • Full AI agent library (10+ agents)
  • Predictive analytics and recommendations
  • Industry-leading coaching AI
  • Platform recognized as "operating system" not just software

D. New Revenue Models

  • Success-based pricing proven and scaled
  • Coaching and events as revenue streams
  • Premium services beyond software
  • Partnership tier becoming high-margin business

Phase 3 Key Deliverables

  • Market leader position established
  • Scaled coaching and partnership operations
  • New revenue models contributing meaningfully
  • Customer lifetime value 3-5x higher than SaaS baseline

What We're Watching

Risk 1: Attribution Is Unclear

Concern

Can't prove Ailo drives outcomes vs. other factors

Mitigation

Heavy investment in measurement infrastructure first; pilot approach lets us test before scaling

Risk 2: Agencies Don't Value Coaching

Concern

See it as overhead, not value-add

Mitigation

Include coaching in all tiers from day one; make it core to the experience, not optional add-on

Risk 3: Can't Hire Right Coaching Talent

Concern

Need ex-agency principals/operators, not just support staff

Mitigation

Partner with agency consultants initially; build hiring pipeline; strong comp packages

Risk 4: Success-Based Pricing Too Complex

Concern

Hard to sell, hard to administer

Mitigation

Start simple with pilots; only scale what works; keep Core/Accelerate as traditional pricing

Risk 5: Market Perception Doesn't Shift

Concern

Still seen as "just software"

Mitigation

Heavy investment in case studies, thought leadership, events; let customers tell the story

Risk 6: Product Isn't Complete Enough

Concern

Core feature gaps undermine "complete system" positioning

Mitigation

Phase 1 prioritizes completing Core; don't market transformation until foundation is solid
